About us

At Hermès, we believe in the art of craftsmanship—where each creation is a testament to our commitment to excellence, our passion for innovation, and our respect for tradition.

A Journey of Timeless Luxury

Founded in 1837 by Thierry Hermès in the heart of Paris, Hermès began as a harness workshop dedicated to creating custom saddles and horse-drawn carriage accessories for the most discerning members of French aristocracy. From its very first day, Hermès distinguished itself by an unwavering commitment to excellence, craftsmanship, and a meticulous attention to detail.

Hermès Today

At Hermès, we are more than just a luxury brand—we are a way of life. Our maisons and boutiques, scattered across the globe, offer an immersive shopping experience where the art of craftsmanship is at the heart of everything we do. With each collection, we continue to redefine the boundaries of fashion, always guided by our heritage of exceptional quality, timeless elegance, and forward-thinking innovation. From the iconic Kelly bag to the bold patterns of our silk scarves, from the bespoke tailoring of our men's and women's fashion to the artisanal craftsmanship of our fine leather goods, every piece created by Hermès embodies the dedication to excellence that has been our hallmark for over 180 years. These creations are not just products; they are works of art, each reflecting our unwavering commitment to beauty, refinement, and lasting value.

The Iconic Birkin Bag

Since its debut in 1984, the Birkin Bag has become synonymous with luxury, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance. Named after the British actress and singer Jane Birkin, this iconic piece emerged from a chance encounter between Jane and Jean-Louis Dumas, the CEO of Hermès, on a flight from Paris to London. Inspired by Jane’s desire for a practical yet stylish bag to accompany her on her travels, Jean-Louis set out to create a design that would not only meet her needs but redefine the art of handbag making.
